Purrmanent's main features: (1) Onboarding questionnaire that generates a personalized 90-day plan for each cat. (2) Checklists — a daily care board and a 90-day phase-milestone board, organized as a Kanban with To-Do, In-Progress, and Done columns. (3) Gamification — you earn points for completing checklist items and crisis steps, unlock badges, and receive a graduation certificate at day 90. (4) Crisis Mode — guided step-by-step protocols for emergencies like not eating, hiding for days, aggression, lethargy, or trouble urinating, with an AI fallback for situations outside the built-in protocols. (5) Health Log — track vaccinations, deworming, vet visits, weight, and notes, with reminders before due dates. (6) AI Coach — a chat assistant that answers cat-care questions and can take actions for you. (7) Push notifications for daily and health reminders.
