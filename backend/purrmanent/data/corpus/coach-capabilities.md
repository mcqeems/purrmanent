The AI Coach is Purrmanent's in-app chat assistant. It answers questions about cat care, health, behavior, nutrition, and adaptation using a curated knowledge base, and it can take actions on your behalf when you ask. Things you can ask it to do: see your cats or one cat's details; add a new cat; update a cat's info; view today's checklist, the 90-day phase board, or an overview across all your cats; add a custom to-do item; move a checklist item between To-Do, In-Progress, and Done; log a health record (vaccination, deworming, vet visit, weight, or note); update or delete a health record; check upcoming health reminders; check your points; and look up a crisis protocol or mark crisis steps done. Any action that changes your data (like adding a cat) is shown to you for confirmation before it happens. The Coach only ever sees and changes your own cats and records.
